Who is the author of "Voyelles"?

PREFIX symbo:
https://github.com/giorgimariachiara/KRKE/blob/main/SymbOntology/SymbOntology.owl#

SELECT ?Author

WHERE {
symbo:Voyelles symbo:hasAuthor ?Author

CQ10

Which is the most frequent theme in the poems?

PREFIX symbo:
https://github.com/giorgimariachiara/KRKE/blob/main/SymbOntology/SymbOntology.owl#

SELECT ?Theme(COUNT(?Line) as?Theme_count)

WHERE{?Line symbo:express ?Theme.}

GROUP BY ?Theme

ORDER BY DESC(?Theme_count)

LIMIT 1

CQ15

Which is the most frequent figure of speech in "Voyelles"?

PREFIX symbo:
https://github.com/giorgimariachiara/KRKE/blob/main/SymbOntology/SymbOntology.owl#

SELECT ?FigureOfSpeech (COUNT(?Line) as ?FigureOfSpeech_count)

WHERE{?Line symbo:contains ?FigureOfSpeech.

?Line symbo:belongsTo symbo:Voyelles.}

GROUP BY ?FigureOfSpeech

ORDER BY DESC(?FigureOfSpeech_count)
